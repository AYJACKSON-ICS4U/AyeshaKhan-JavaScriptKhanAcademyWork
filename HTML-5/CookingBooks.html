Recipe #2

  • Time: an hour
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
Carrots 1
Butter 1 tbsp
Salt half a tsp
red cilli pepper 1.5 tps
peas 0.5 cup
bell peppers 1
Pasta 1.5 cups
Soya sauce 1 tps

Step 1: boil water and cook pasta.
Step 2: While the pasta cooks in a frying pan add the butter, peas, carrots and bell peppers, and bring to a simmer adding in the salt and red pepper and soya sauce.
Step 3: Fry till crispy and add a tsp of black pepper and chopped green chillipeppers.
Step 4: after the pasta has cooked, drain it of the water and place it in a sauce pan adding the mixture that you had fried.
Step 5: Add a touch more butter for flavour and stir fry for about 2 minutes.
